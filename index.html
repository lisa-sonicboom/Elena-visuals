About Elena

I believe every photograph should feel like a held breath.

With over a decade behind the lens, I've built a practice rooted in quiet observation and honest emotion. My work spans intimate weddings in countryside estates, editorial portraits for global brands, and personal lifestyle stories across six continents.

I shoot with available light whenever possible — drawn to the way golden hour bends through linen curtains or the way rain-wet cobblestones mirror a stolen glance.
